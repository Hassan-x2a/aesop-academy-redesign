How Certifications Work

  
    
    
  • 🧩Module Points Only — 1 point per completed module, across every course on the platform. Finish all lessons in a module to earn the point. Points alone determine your level.
    • 
    
  • 🏫Foundations is Optional — Complete the AI Foundations course to deepen your understanding and build a stronger portfolio. Your certification level progresses independently based on module points.
    • 
    
  • 📈Levels never expire. Points accumulate permanently. You keep every level you reach.
    • 
    
  • 🎖️Your badge appears in the top nav, on your Student Profile, and in every course hub you open — a visible mark of how far you've come.
    • 
    
  • 📄Resume-ready credentials — each level carries a formal certificate title you can list on LinkedIn, a résumé, or a job application.
    • 
    
  • 🦅Level 9 (Master) and Level 10 (Architect) are rare. Architect is the highest rank on the platform.
