# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Aesop serves learners, educators, school leaders, workforce partners, and internal builders who need practical AI education tools. They are usually trying to place a learner, guide practice, inspect progress, review course or certification evidence, or move quickly through a learning workflow.

## Product Purpose

Aesop AI Academy helps people learn, practice, and prove AI skills through courses, guided conversations, assessments, certification evidence, and transcript-style records. Success means the interface feels trustworthy, legible, and useful under real work conditions, not merely decorative.

## Brand Personality

Serious, literate, practical, and quietly ambitious. Aesop should feel like an expert learning institution with modern AI fluency: high-trust, direct, and capable.

## Anti-references

- No aqua, teal, cyan, or green-blue accent family on product surfaces.
- No random pastel card palettes.
- No “AI SaaS default” purple-blue gradient wash.
- No light text on light surfaces or dark text on dark surfaces.
- No decorative cards inside cards.
- No styling that makes a task UI feel like a marketing hero.
- No purely technical “contrast pass” that leaves the product visually incoherent.

## Design Principles

1. Product first: every screen should help the user do the next useful thing.
2. Coherent surfaces: panels should belong to one product system, not look like unrelated widgets.
3. Meaningful color: use color to signal structure, state, priority, or brand identity.
4. High-trust readability: text, controls, labels, and transcript evidence must be readable at a glance.
5. Visual review before shipping: a browser screenshot and design critique are part of the work, not optional polish.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast for normal and large text. Respect reduced motion. Do not rely on color alone for state. Product controls need visible focus states, readable placeholders, and predictable interaction patterns.
